About Me

Thanks for visiting my web page. I'm an aspiring software developer currently based in Manchester, in the UK. I don't have a technical background; I studied History at undergraduate level and then got a Masters degree in Politics. A couple of years ago, however, I discovered a love for technology. Since then I've been working on developing my technical skills. I've done this in my free time. I have a full time job working for the Cooperative in their Food Supply Chain department.

For the last year, I've focused on web development technologies, particularly JavaScript. I've gained experience of using the React and jQuery libraries, and new ES6 features. I have experience of using of HTML and CSS, including Bootstrap. In addition to this, I use Git and Bash. I also have a good knowledge of SQL, and I love working with data.

Please take a look at my projects. There's more coming soon! You can also view my LinkedIn profile here, and my GitHub profile here.
